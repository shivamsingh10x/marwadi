FAQs

Is there any Loan Facility Available ?

Yes, the university has tie-ups with several banks to facilitate education loans for students. You can contact the admission office for more details and assistance with the loan application process.

Is there any Scholarship Available ?

Absolutely. We offer a range of merit-based and need-based scholarships. Please visit the 'Scholarships' section on our website for detailed criteria and application deadlines.

How is the Placement at Marwadi University ?

Marwadi University has a dedicated placement cell that works tirelessly to bring top companies to campus. We have a consistent track record of excellent placements across various industries.

How to Reach MU ?

The university is well-connected by road. It is located on the Rajkot-Morbi Highway. You can easily reach us via public transport, taxi, or private vehicle. Our location is also available on Google Maps.

Is there a Hostel Facility available at Marwadi University ?

Yes, we provide separate, fully-equipped hostel facilities for both boys and girls with all modern amenities including Wi-Fi, laundry services, and 24/7 security.

What is the Fee Structure of Degree Programmes at Marwadi University ?

The fee structure varies depending on the program. You can find detailed fee information for each course on its respective page on our official website or in the university brochure.
